A — Two place inputs (From + To)

Transport shows two GooglePlacesInputs: origin and destination. Both capture lat/lng. The map draws a segment directly from origin to destination. Other event types keep one place input.

Transport Stay Ticket Food
Flight GRU → NRT
📍 From: Guarulhos Airport, São Paulo
Destination (text): Tokyo
📅 2026-03-15
🕐 08:00

B — Origin with Places + destination as text

From uses GooglePlacesInput (with lat/lng for map), destination is a plain text field. Simpler form, but destination won't show as a pin on the map.

Transport Stay Ticket Food
Flight GRU → NRT
📍 Guarulhos Airport
FROM
↓
📍 Narita Airport
TO
🛫 Dep: 08:00
🛬 Arr: 23:00
📅 Date: 2026-03-15

C — Two places + separate departure/arrival times

Like A but also captures arrival time separately. Useful for flights with time zones. Most complete but most complex form.
